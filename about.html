About Us














Love It, Live It

We are a gaming organization based around the world with
a dream of making a name for ourselves in the eSports community.
We Believe that anyone no matter what their religion, gender,
sexual orientation, or Age should be able to play at a semi competitive level.








Our History

We started out in March of 2018 as Ninja Turtle eSports but disbanded
shortly after. It wasnt until November of 2019 when we
rentered the eSports scene, but this time under a different name, Darkness Ensues.
After a ton of changes and moves, including a merge with ReaP Gaming, we are who
we are today, an multi-game truly international eSports organization made to be the best.
